About

Developer

Philipp Amana is the developer behind Spointed. With a simple background in Software Architecture, and the little desire to understand Data Science, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning capabilities as well as how these can be utilised in solving real life problems in the world with sustainable technology while leveraging technology skills, experience and clarity plus the passion to learn and solve real-world challenges; Philipp decided to work on an idea (Restaurant's Business Location Recommendation System) and form it into a system for aspiring restaurant owners. The approach of the system ensures that aspiring or existing restaurant owners find the optimal location for setting up their business operations, fostering success and growth in a desired and beneficial market.

App

Spointed is a Restaurant's Business Location Recommendation System designed to provide a personalized location based on the provided insights, whether you're starting a new restaurant business or looking to expand, Spointed help you find the optimal location to match your restaurant needs based on your budget. The model combines comprehensive search capabilities with simpler analytics to ensure that you make the required decision-making process about setting up your restaurant's business operations in the optimal location.

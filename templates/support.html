How to Use Our Business Location Recommendation System (BLRS)

1. Getting Started

To begin using our BLRS, follow these steps:

  • Visit the homepage and either sign up for a new account or log in if you already have an account.
  • Once logged in, youâ€™ll be directed to your dashboard where you can start setting up your profile.

2. Setting Up Your Profile

To ensure accurate recommendations, set up your profile with the following details:

  • Business name, type, and description
  • Preferred locations and target market
  • Any specific requirements or preferences you might have

Navigate to your profile settings from the top-right corner and enter these details.

3. Exploring Locations

Use the search and filter options to find potential locations for your business:

  • Enter keywords related to your business type and preferred location.
  • Use filters to narrow down results based on factors like demographics, competition, and accessibility.

Explore the recommendations and view details on each location.

4. Analyzing Recommendations

Review the detailed reports provided for each recommended location:

  • Understand the market potential and demographic information.
  • Compare factors like rental costs, foot traffic, and proximity to competitors.

Use these insights to determine which locations best meet your business needs.

5. Saving and Comparing Locations

Make use of our saving and comparison features:

  • Save locations you're interested in to a personal list for easy access.
  • Use the comparison tool to evaluate multiple locations side-by-side based on various criteria.

This will help you make an informed decision when selecting the best location for your business.

6. Seeking Assistance

If you need help or have questions:

  • Check our FAQ section for common queries.
  • Contact our support team through the contact page or email us directly at support@situated.com.
